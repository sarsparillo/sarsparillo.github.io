About The Author

Whalley Thomas grew up with stories as babysitters, spending afternoons in the back room of his mother’s bookstores and his weekends losing himself in his father’s extensive collection of old and weird science fiction and fantasy novels. It was only a matter of time before he started making his own.

Proudly queer and a joyous disaster, Whalley's works are full of rich, diverse characters trying their best to hold everything together, and rarely succeeding. He is as yet unpublished, but this page will be updated as soon as there’s something to update!

When not writing endlessly weird and melodramatic fantasy stories, Whalley is often found swinging tools for odd wood projects, keeping his cat and dog from tearing his house apart, or arguing over why he should be allowed to helm a Final Fantasy 8 remake. It neeeeeeds one, okay.
